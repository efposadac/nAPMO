compute_1body_ints()¶

Computes one boy molecular integrals for all quantum species in the system, including:

  • Overlap
  • Kinetic
  • Nuclear potential
compute_energy()¶

Computes the total energy for a multi-species system.

Computes the density guess using the HCORE method: \(H C = e S C\)
