analytical_integration¶

analytical_integration.kronecker_delta(a, b)¶

Calculates the delta of Kronecker for a and b.

\(\delta_{ij} = 0\) if \(i \neq j\), otherwise, \(\delta_{ij} = 1\)

Parameters:
  • a (int) – i in \(\delta_{ij}\)
  • b (int) – j in \(\delta_{ij}\)
analytical_integration.obaraSaika_recursion(PA, PB, gamma, l_a, l_b)¶

Perform the Obara-Saika (1988) recursion to calculate the overlap integral:

\(<\phi_A|\phi_B>\)

Where each \(\phi\) corresponds to a GTO PrimitiveGaussian

Parameters:
  • PA (numpy.ndarray(3)) – Origin of \(\phi_A\)
  • PB (numpy.ndarray(3)) – Origin of \(\phi_B\)
  • gamma (float64) – Reduced exponent (see: Gaussian product theorem)
  • l_a (int) – Angular moment index of \(\phi_A\)
  • l_b (int) – Angular moment index of \(\phi_B\)
Returns:

x, y, z – x, y, and z components of the recursion.
Return type:

tuple
